# 📦 Inventory & Supply Chain Analytics

An end-to-end **Power BI dashboard project** analyzing inventory levels,
supply chain performance, and order fulfillment across regions and product categories.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes inventory and supply chain data to uncover insights around
warehouse utilization, inventory turnover, transportation costs, lead times,
and order fulfillment status. The goal is to help businesses optimize stock levels,
reduce backorders, and improve supply chain efficiency.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

- **Units Sold by Year** — Sales grew sharply from ~1K (2021) to ~195K-198K (2022-2024)
- **Transportation Cost by Region & Category** — West region has the highest transportation cost, led by Furniture and Clothing
- **Lead Time by Category** — Fairly even across categories, ranging between 15.29 to 16.60 days
- **Inventory Level by Category & Region** — Clothing and Furniture carry the highest inventory across all regions

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Fulfilled orders** vastly outnumber pending and canceled orders, indicating strong order processing
- **West region** incurs the highest transportation costs, especially for Furniture
- High **Inventory Turnover Ratio (23.47x)** suggests efficient inventory management
- **Warehouse Utilization at 34.1%** indicates available capacity for scaling operations
